Welcome to Phnom Penh

About Phnom Penh

Phnom Penh is the captial and most populus city of Cambodia. It has been the national captial since the French Protecture of Cambodia, and has grown to become the nation's economic, industrial and cultural centre. Once Once known as the "Pearl of Asia", it was considered one of the loveliest French-built cities in Indochina. Phnom Penh is the significant global and domestic tourist destinations for Cambodia.

As of 2019, Phnom Penh had a population of 2,129,371 people, with a total population density of 3,136 inhabitants per square kilometre in a 679 square kilometres (262 sq mi) city area. The population growth rate of the city is 3.92%.Phnom Penh is Cambodia's economic centre as it accounts for a large portion of the Cambodian economy. Double-digit economic growth rates in recent years have triggered an economic boom in Phnom Penh, with new hotels, restaurants, schools, bars, high rises and residential buildings springing up in the city.

Tourist Attraction

1. Royal Palace

The highlight of Phnom Penh is the beautiful Royal Palace - the seat and residence of Cambodia's royal family since the 1860s. The spired-roof pavilions of the complex are an excellent example of classic Khmer architecture.

2. Cambodia National Musuem

The traditional Khmer building that houses the national collection was built in 1920. Inside, the dazzling array of ancient Khmer artistry, with more than 1,800 objects on display, is a must visit for anyone with an interest in Cambodian history.

3. Choeung Ek

The Killing Fields of Choeung Ek are a somber reminder of the terror of the Khmer Rouge whose brutal regime, intending to turn Cambodia into a socialist agrarian society, ruled the country between 1975 and early 1979. In under four years, it's estimated that between one and three million Cambodians were killed under Pol Pot's authoritarian command.

Popular Food in Phnom Penh

Typical Cambodian Sandwich

This sandwich is typically served with meaty ingredients such as pate, ham or pork. It is generally topped with cucumber, carrots, chives, and onions. It costs around 0.75$ to 1.5$ .

Pork and Rice

Pork and Rice is one of the popular dishes in Cambodia which mostly people like to serve it as breakfast. It is cheap, fast and taste delicious. Once you visit Cambodia, you must not forget to try this kind of food. It costs around 1$ to 1.5$ per dish.

Street Food Barbecue

Once you visit Phnom Penh, you will see this type of food is available everywher in Phnom Penh. Combining the ingredients and meetball or beef toogether will provide uniquely taste. The price is around 0.5$ to 1$ per chin.
